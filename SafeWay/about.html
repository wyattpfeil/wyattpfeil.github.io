Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

SafeWay Technologies has been built around the PhoneSafe, a device that aims to put an end to texting and driving. According to the National Safety Council, over 390,000 injuries occur each year due to automobile accidents caused by distracted phone usage. With this product, we are targeting all drivers looking to improve their safety while operating a motor vehicle. As of 2019, there are 229 million licensed drivers in the United States and we aim to sell our product to each of these consumers. Because this device greatly reduces the risk of accidents, PhoneSafe is valuable to many including drivers, pedestrians, and vehicle insurance companies. With recent advances in 3d printing technology, we will be able to produce this at an extremely affordable rate and retain high profit margins while still maintaining accessibility to most drivers in the United States. The PhoneSafe is beneficial to all drivers and should be adopted by all persons operating motor vehicles in order to improve safety and drastically decrease distracted driving.
